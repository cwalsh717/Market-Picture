About Market Picture

Market Picture is a daily market weather report. Open it up, see whether markets are in Risk-On, Risk-Off, or Mixed mode, read a short plain-English summary of what's happening, and get on with your day. Think of it like checking the weather — but for financial markets.

No jargon walls. No paywalls. No signup required. Just a clear, data-driven snapshot of what's moving across stocks, bonds, currencies, commodities, and crypto — updated throughout the day.

How It Works

Market Picture pulls live data from across major asset classes — equities, interest rates, currencies, commodities, crypto, and more. It runs that data through a set of rules to determine the current market regime: are conditions generally favorable for risk assets, unfavorable, or somewhere in between?

Then, twice a day, AI generates a short narrative explaining what's happening and why — in plain English anyone can understand. No opinions, no predictions. Just the facts of what the data is showing.
